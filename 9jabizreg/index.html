What We Offer

Pre-Incorporation

  • Business Name Registration

    • Simple and affordable form of registration
    • Requires only one or two proprietors
    • No separation between owner and business (not a legal entity)
    • Ideal for small enterprises, freelancers, traders, artisans, and startups

  • Company Registration

    • Private Limited Company (Ltd): Most common, for small and medium businesses.
    • Public Limited Company (PLC): For larger corporations offering shares to the public
    • Company Limited by Guarantee: Usually for non-profits or charitable organizations.

  • Incorporated Trustees

    Incorporated Trustees is a legal structure used in Nigeria to register non-profit organizations, such as NGOs, churches, foundations, clubs, and associations, under Part F of the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC).

  • Trademark Registration

    To give the owner exclusive rights to use the registered mark and prevent others from using identical or confusingly similar marks in the same industry or market.

  • SCUML Registration

    To ensure that Designated Non-Financial Businesses and Professions (DNFBPs) comply with anti-money laundering (AML) and counter-terrorism financing (CTF) laws.

  • TIN Registration

    To legally identify a business or individual as a taxpayer and ensure compliance with Nigeria’s tax laws, including Value Added Tax (VAT), Company Income Tax (CIT), and Personal Income Tax (PIT).
